# KAVACH Operator Quickstart - Field Essentials

This guide covers the essential field actions for KAVACH operators. **Field usability > Technical purity.**

---

## 1. Access & Security
* **Login**: Use your PNO (Personnel Number) and Password.
* **Token Expired**: If the app logs you out, re-authenticate immediately. All offline work is preserved locally.
* **Device Revoked**: If you see "Security Restriction," your device has been revoked by the Command Center. Contact your CO immediately.

## 2. Mission Operations
* **Create Incident**: Tap the **(+) Incident** button. Capture media, add GPS, and describe the field reality. Tap **Submit** to queue for sync.
* **Broadcasts**: Check the **Broadcast Center** for real-time alerts from Command. These are high-priority orders.
* **Approvals**: If you need to change a restricted field (e.g., officer role), a **DraftChange** will be created and sent to your CO for ratification.

## 3. Synchronization & Connectivity
* **Sync Status**: 
  - ✅ **System Operational**: All data is synchronized.
  - ⚠️ **Sync Delayed**: Network is slow; data is queued locally.
  - ❌ **Network Unstable**: You are offline. Work will sync automatically when signal returns.
* **Conflict Resolution**: If your update is marked as **CONFLICTED**, it means someone else changed the data first. Review the "CO Override" in your activity timeline.

## 4. Emergency Steps
* **Ngrok Failure**: If the app stays offline despite good internet, the tunnel may be down. Contact IT for the new "Service URL."
* **Lost Device**: Immediately report to the Command Center to trigger **Remote Revocation** of your session and device keys.

## 5. Daily Maintenance
* **Sync Before Shift**: Ensure all data is synced before entering a known dead-zone.
* **Battery**: KAVACH is optimized for field work, but keep a power bank for 12hr+ operations.
